Welcome to the Clothes Warehouse Management System!

About This Website

This website is designed to help you manage the inventory of a warehouse by adding new items, viewing existing items, and sorting them by brand.

How to Use the Website

The website has 3 (three) main pages, each with its own set of buttons that provide different functionalities:

Warehouse Mgmt System

  • Add a New Item: Click on the "Add New Item" button to enter details of a new item such as its name, brand, year of creation, and price.
  • View Items: Click on the "View Item List" button to see all items stored in the warehouse. You can sort the list by brand.

Add Item

  • Add a New Item: Click on the "Add Item" button to add the item, based on all of the details of your item.
  • Back to Home: Click on the "Back to Home" button to go back to the home page.

Items List

  • Apply Filter: Click on the "Apply Filter" button to filter the items by brand and by year.
  • Clear Filters: Click on the "Clear Filters" button to clear the filters and show all of the items.
  • Sort 2022 Items: Click on the "Display 2022 Items Sorted by Brand" button to display only items from 2022 sorted by brand.
  • Add a New Item: Click on the "Add New Item" button to enter details of a new item such as its name, brand, year of creation, and price.
  • Back to Home: Click on the "Back to Home" button to go back to the home page.
